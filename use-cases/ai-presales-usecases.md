# AI Use Cases for Sales Engineering

## 1. AI-assisted discovery
Generate tailored discovery questions based on prospect profile and business context.

## 2. POC preparation
Use AI to structure scope, expected outcomes, risks, and success criteria.

## 3. Demo preparation
Adapt demos to a specific vertical, stakeholder group, or customer pain point.

## 4. Technical follow-up generation
Draft structured post-meeting follow-ups with action items and next steps.

## 5. Competitive positioning support
Use AI to simulate competitor messaging and prepare differentiated responses.
