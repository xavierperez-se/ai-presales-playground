# Voice AI Use Cases – Sales Engineering Perspective

## 1. AI Voice for Customer Engagement
Use realistic AI-generated voices to personalize outbound communication at scale.

Example:
- localized voice messages for prospects
- human-like tone adapted to industry

## 2. Demo Enhancement
Replace static demos with dynamic voice interactions:
- AI narrating product demos
- interactive scenarios

## 3. Training & Enablement
Generate realistic role-play scenarios:
- simulate customer objections
- train sales teams with AI voice personas

## 4. Customer Support Automation
AI voice agents handling:
- first-level support
- FAQs
- routing

## 5. Storytelling in Presales
Use voice AI to:
- deliver narratives
- create emotional engagement
- differentiate from standard demos

## Why it matters
Voice adds a new layer to AI:
- emotion
- realism
- engagement

This creates stronger impact in presales conversations.
