# Prompt Library – Sales Engineering

## 1. Discovery Assistant
Generate structured discovery questions based on the prospect's context, industry, and stakeholders.

## 2. POC Success Criteria Builder
Help define measurable and realistic success criteria before launching a technical evaluation.

## 3. Demo Personalization
Adapt a demo narrative to a specific customer context, business challenge, and audience.

## 4. Technical Objection Handling
Simulate customer objections around security, integration, complexity, or cost, and propose structured responses.

## 5. Job Fit Analysis
Evaluate whether a role is aligned with a Senior Sales Engineer trajectory focused on SaaS, AI, and cybersecurity.
